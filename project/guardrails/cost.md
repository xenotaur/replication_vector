# Cost Guardrails

## Scope Cost
- Favor a small playable MVP over broad content expansion.
- Keep one primary resource in the initial game loop.
- Limit MVP enemies to a small set with clear tactical roles.
- Defer galactic maps, tech trees, elaborate story, multiplayer, complex save/load, and large asset pipelines.

## Maintenance Cost
- Keep project-local scripts and CI simple until reuse pressure is proven.
- Extract shared Velumin helpers only after stable repeated needs appear.
- Avoid premature abstractions around gameplay systems before prototype behavior is validated.
